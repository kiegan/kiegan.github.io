## Professional 

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Statistics at [Iowa State University](https://www.stat.iastate.edu/).  

I earned my BA from [St. Olaf College](https://wp.stolaf.edu/) in 2015, with majors in Mathematics and Spanish and a Statistics concentration.  

I earned my MSc in Statistics from Iowa State University in December 2017. My creative component project was [A User-Friendly U.S. Census Data Browser](https://kiegan.shinyapps.io/get_your_data/).  

My research interests include data visualization, statistical graphics, data journalism, statistical learning, and [statistical applications in forensic evidence](https://forensicstats.org/).
